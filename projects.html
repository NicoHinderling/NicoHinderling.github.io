Sim Pencil AI


Sim is a game where players take turns drawing lines from point to point. The object of the game is to avoid making a triangle with your own lines.


Sim Pencil AI was a project that originally began in one of my Computer Science courses at Boston University during my first year. While most of the things we had assigned were less exciting, I found the concept of Artifical Intelligence fastinating. This was also a wonderful example for applications of MinMax trees!
